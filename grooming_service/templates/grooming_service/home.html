About Us

At Woof & Wash Grooming, we are passionate about providing top-quality grooming services for your beloved pets.

Our team of experienced groomers is dedicated to ensuring that your pet receives the care and attention they deserve.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to make grooming a positive experience for every pet. We use gentle techniques and high-quality products to ensure the best care.

We offer a wide range of services, including bathing, grooming, nail trimming, and ear cleaning, all tailored to meet the unique needs of your pet.

Our state-of-the-art facility is designed to provide a safe and comfortable environment, ensuring a stress-free experience for your furry friends.

We believe in continuous learning and regularly update our skills and knowledge to stay current with the latest grooming trends and techniques.
